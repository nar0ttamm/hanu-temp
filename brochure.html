OUR PRINTING PROCESS

We utilize advanced printing technologies to ensure the highest quality results for your team apparel. Each method offers unique benefits to suit different requirements and preferences.

Sublimation Printing

Our premium printing method uses heat to transfer dye onto the fabric at a molecular level. The result is a full-color, vibrant design that will never crack, peel, or fade, even after multiple washes.

  • Unlimited colors and design possibilities
  • Breathable fabric with no heavy ink layer
  • Permanent designs that won't fade or crack
  • Ideal for complete custom jerseys and complex designs

Screen Printing

Our traditional screen printing process delivers bold, vibrant colors and exceptional durability. Perfect for team uniforms that need to withstand the rigors of athletic competition.

  • Vivid, opaque colors with excellent coverage
  • Extremely durable and long-lasting
  • Cost-effective for larger quantities
  • Classic, professional finish

Heat Transfer

Our precision heat transfer process allows for detailed graphics and text to be applied to virtually any fabric. This versatile method is perfect for names, numbers, and logos.

  • Precise application of complex designs
  • Excellent option for small batches or individual customization
  • Quick turnaround time
  • Great for adding details to pre-existing garments

Embroidery

For a premium, professional look, our embroidery service creates dimensional designs with exceptional durability and a high-end appearance.

  • Professional, prestigious appearance
  • Extremely durable and long-lasting
  • Ideal for logos, team names, and smaller designs
  • Perfect for jackets, hats, and off-field apparel
